  • Lamb Over Rice

    • This was inspired from Lamb over rice from the halal carts in NYC. A halal cart is a food cart which serves halal Mediterranean cuisine. During quarantine I missed being able to eat lamb over rice, so I tried to make my own at home with the help of Trini Cooking with Natasha. Cutting the meat up and sauteeing it on a griddle pan gives you more of an authentic outcome. You can find griddle pan on my products page.
