About Me

Hello, my name is Simone Romear and I'm from West Palm Beach, Florida. I have lived in Brooklyn for the past 10 years. I love to cook and I am always trying new recipes. My roomate is a chef and she helps me refine my skills. During quarantine I had the time to experiment, which was a lot of fun. There were a lot of foods that I missed being able to go eat... So I tried to make some of them at home!
